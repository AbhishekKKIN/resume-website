About us

Print CV is a brainchild of couple of vibrant minds Nishant Bharadwaj and Nitin Vats. They are talented recruiters having specialities in hiring mid to CEO level candidates across Europe, Middle East and the USA.

Print CV is a large team of recruiters, employers and CEOs across the world. They are our advisors, affiliates and board members.

Our History

Through their experience they understood most good candidates lose out big opportunities just because they lack the art of expressing their talent with the help of their CV. Similarly most recruiters tend to ignore such candidates and effectively lose out an opportunity hiring the best available talent.

Can there be a solution which can help candidates to write clean and well- designed CV?

In pursuit of arriving at right solution for this problem Nishant and Nitin laid the foundation for Print CV.

Who we are

We are a team of 1500+ recruiters, domain experts, developers and designers. Our goal is to make hiring simple. We endeavour to kill the glitches and make hiring simple.

For candidates we save time. For recruiters we bring consistency. Our goal is to simplify hiring. Bring structure to resume and cover letter writing.

What is a Cover Letter?

A resume is a formal document that a job applicant creates to itemize his or her qualifications for a position.

A resume is usually accompanied by a customized cover letter in which the applicant expresses an interest in a specific job or company and draws attention to the most relevant specifics on the resume.

FAQs about Cover Letters

What should I include on my cover letter?

Everyone’s cover letter will include different things due to varying work experience and qualifications. However, if you’re wondering what to put on your cover letter, there are some basic requirements that you should include.

At a minimum, cover letters must have the following standard components:

  • Contact information
  • cover letter introduction
  • cover letter education section
  • Work experience
  • Relevant cover letter skills & certifications
